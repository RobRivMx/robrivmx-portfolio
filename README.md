## 🏗️ Architectural Decision Record (ADR): Why Frontend-Only?

You might notice this portfolio repository operates entirely without a traditional backend. This is a deliberate architectural decision:
* **Zero Cold Starts:** By keeping the interactive demos (InsightAI, DocBrain, AutoFlow) purely on the client-side, technical recruiters and engineering managers can experience the UI/UX and AI logic instantly, without waiting for sleeping server instances to wake up.
* **Edge Performance:** Showcasing the raw power of modern React (Vite) and browser-based processing.
* **Security & Agility:** Demonstrating a lightweight, decoupled approach perfect for rapid prototyping and "Vibe Coding" methodologies.

## 🎨 UI/UX Engineering: The Neumorphic Experience

The entire platform is built upon a custom **Matte Black & Neon Glass** Neumorphic design system.
